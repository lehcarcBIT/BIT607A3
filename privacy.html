Our privacy policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • make appointment bookings or respond to queries

Besides our staff, we share this information with:

  • debt collection agencies in order to pursue unpaid accounts.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter an email or phone number, we'll be unable to confirm appointment bookings.


You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at help@snazzypaws.com
